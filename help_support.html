How do I invite a subcontractor to a specific project?

Go to Project Settings > Team > Invite Member. Enter their email and select "Subcontractor" as the role to limit their access to specific folders.

How do I export a Daily Report as a PDF?

Navigate to the 'Reports' tab, select the date range, and click the 'Export' button. Select 'PDF' format to generate a printable version.
